# Interview Questions

Top picks for a short interview (3-4 questions max):

1. **"Walk me through your last launch — idea to live."** — proves they ship, reveals speed and taste
2. **"What's your AI tooling stack?"** — confirms they're AI-native, not AI-curious
3. **"What would you do in your first week as a member?"** — separates contributors from lurkers
4. **"What's a popular take in the builder space you disagree with?"** — reveals independent thinking vs. trend chasing
